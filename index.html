Before buying, ask yourself:

Do you really need it?

Don’t buy it!

Do you only need it once?

Can you borrow it?

Borrow it!

Can you rent it?

Rent it!

Can you buy it second-hand?

Buy it second-hand!

Can you find a sustainable business that sells it?

Buy it from a sustainable business!

Can you find a small local company selling it?

Buy it from a small local company!

Can you find it in a style and quality that will last for a long time?

Buy it in a style and quality that will last for a long time!

Can you buy it from a small, non-local company?

Buy it from a small, non-local company!

Can you make it?

Make it!

Do you really, really need it?

Buy it!

Don’t buy!
